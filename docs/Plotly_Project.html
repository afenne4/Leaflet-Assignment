Happiest Countries over Time

This presentation uses data collected from the Gallup World Poll and contains information about many countries around the world. It includes information on their happiness, GDP per capita, and a number of other predictors.

I will be focused on how happiness changes in the happiest countries over a certain time period.

Read in and prepare the dataset

data<-read.csv("world-happiness-report.csv")
colnames(data)[1:3]<-c("Country","Year","Happiness")
data<-na.omit(data)

Selecting the 5 happiest countries on average

happycountry<-data%>%
    group_by(Country)%>%
    summarise(avghappy=mean(Happiness))%>%
    arrange(desc(avghappy))
happiest<-happycountry[1:5,]
happydat<-data[data$Country%in%happiest$Country,]
